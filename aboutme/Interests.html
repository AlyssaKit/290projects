Recipe for German Pancakes

  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup flour
  1. Put the butter in a 9x9 inch glass pan. Turn oven on to 375 degrees farenheit. Put the pan with butter in it into the oven.
  2. Crack the eggs into a large bowl and whisk till combined.
  3. Add in milk and whisk till combined.
  4. Add in the flour a little bit at a time and mix until there are no lumps.
  5. Once oven is preheated take out glass pan and set it on a heat safe counter. pour in the batter and then put it back into the oven.
  6. Leave it in the oven for 20 minutes, then take out and enjoy with your choice of toppings. Some toppings I would recommend are: powdered sugar and lemon juice, peanut butter and jam, or just maple syrup.
